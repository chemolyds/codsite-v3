# SOCC '23

---

The Spring Open Chemistry Competition (SOCC) serves as our mock USNCO.
It does not sport our tier system, but instead has a single difficulty that is slightly harder than the predicted USCNO exam.
The scores can be found [here](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/17WhjDgZTGLm1tVDTiRSCwhqg_NjdYAiu6Ghf1hWqdsw/edit?usp=sharing).
