# SOCC '24

---

The Spring Open Chemistry Competition (SOCC) is a mock USNCO.
It does not sport our tier system, but instead has a single difficulty that is slightly harder than the predicted USCNO exam.

The scores can be found [here](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1UU-I7mGhmcmysZh8mBz-Ri5lgsz6X22xn6qoxuFEN-M/edit?usp=sharing).
