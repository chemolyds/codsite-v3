# WCC '23

---
Winter Chemistry Competition is our team competition.
This competition tested both individual chemistry skills and teamwork skills under pressure and boasted a live leaderboard.
Bronze team scores can be found [here](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1nOokmEhvtnbABFDvd4UQjyhc_L2RLzPR0GJcsY-JWbQ/edit?usp=sharing).
Silver team scores can be found [here](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1oJrEVwi-_xb9LnO7xZMmG43DVqJbcUDG_ky4fdVqCk0/edit?usp=sharing).
Gold team scores can be found [here](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1nBcI9JYfoCY1rjcdIF4eqg3GtbcllmRtDKZryY9DpyE/edit?usp=sharing).
