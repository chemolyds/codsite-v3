# WCC '24

---
Winter Chemistry Competition is our team competition.
This competition tested both individual and team skills and has a live graded scoreboard.

Final scores can be found [here](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1cGHBxkN4Ie6-yq0HpcDlQhxZ9SKwYz1tzvAanwx8-Y4/edit?usp=sharing).
